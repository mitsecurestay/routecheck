🚌 Transit Access
Walk (5 min) → Ride On 26 bus → Glenmont Metro → Red Line (no transfer)
The Ride On Route 26 stops near Layhill Rd & Bel Pre Rd, a short walk from the property, and runs directly to Glenmont station — the Red Line terminus. The same Route 26 bus also passes North Bethesda station, directly across the street from NRC Headquarters on Rockville Pike. Once on the Red Line at Glenmont, it's a direct ride with no transfer to NIH (Medical Center station), Union Station, Judiciary Square, Gallery Place, Metro Center, Farragut North, and Dupont Circle.
